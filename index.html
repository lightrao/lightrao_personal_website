Summary 个人简介

Hello everyone, I'm a software engineer. Code, algorithms, and system design are my areas of expertise, driven by a pure curiosity and passion for mathematics and computer science. For me, writing code and solving complex problems isn't just a job; it's more like a practice—immersing myself and honing skills in the world of logic brings a sense of calm.

Beyond technology, Buddhism offers another realm for wisdom. It's not a religious label but a practical life philosophy, teaching me to maintain mindfulness amidst complexity, cultivate resilience in the face of challenges, and find my own rhythm and balance between work and life. This perspective helps me appreciate the process, rather than solely focusing on results.

I enjoy challenges and collaborating with like-minded individuals. Of course, like everyone else, I sometimes feel a bit lost in the vast field of technology or at life's crossroads. However, this precisely reminds me to reflect, adjust, and gain a clearer understanding of my path forward.

大家好，我是一名软件工程师。代码、算法和系统设计是我的专业领域，而驱动我深入其中的，是对数学与计算机科学那份纯粹的好奇与热爱。对我而言，写代码、解难题，不仅仅是工作，更像是一种修行——在逻辑的世界里沉潜、打磨，能让心绪逐渐归于平静。

技术之外，佛学是我汲取智慧的另一片天地。它并非信仰的标签，而是一种生活哲学的实践，教会我在纷繁中保持正念，在挑战面前多一份韧性，在工作与生活间找到属于自己的节奏与平衡点。这种视角让我更能欣赏过程，而非仅仅执着于结果。

我喜欢挑战，也享受与思维同频的人交流碰撞。当然，和大家一样，偶尔我也会在技术的广阔天地或人生的路口感到一丝迷茫，但这恰恰提醒我去反思、去调整，去更清晰地认识自己前进的方向。
